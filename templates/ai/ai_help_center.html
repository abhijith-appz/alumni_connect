How do I search for alumni by company?

Use the Alumni Search page and filter by company name. You can also use the AI Alumni Finder for natural language searches.

How do I apply for a job posted by an alumni?

Open the job listing, scroll to the application form, fill in the details, and click Apply Now.

How does the AI career guidance work?

The AI analyses your profile, department, and interests to suggest tailored career paths and skill recommendations.

How do I send a message to an alumni?

Visit the alumni profile page and click the Message button. Your message will appear in their inbox.

Can I register for events through the platform?

Yes! Go to Events, find the event you want, and click Register. You will receive a confirmation.

How do I update my profile?

Go to Profile or Edit Profile in your dashboard. Update the fields and click Save Changes.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings and find the Account and Security section to change your password.
